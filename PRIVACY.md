# Privacy Policy

Antarman is an anonymous academic support companion. We collect only what is needed to help you track your emotional journey during exam preparation.

## What We Store

- **Anonymous ID:** A random UUID generated in your browser. It is not linked to your email, phone, or any real-world identity.
- **Profile (optional):** Your first name or nickname, exam type, and optional target date.
- **Journal entries:** The text you write during journaling sessions, plus derived emotional metadata (sentiment, themes, triggers) once processed.
- **Insights:** Detected patterns in your emotional trends over time.

## Where Data Lives

- **MongoDB:** Persistent storage for your profile, journal entries, and insights — linked only to your anonymous UUID.
- **Redis:** Short-lived cache for session context and rate limiting. Data expires automatically (4–24 hours).

## What We Do Not Collect

- No email addresses, phone numbers, or passwords.
- No device fingerprints or location tracking.
- No social media or third-party account linking.

## How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to power your personal wellness insights and conversational companion. It is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose beyond your own experience in this app.

## Your Control

Because there are no accounts, your anonymous ID lives in your browser's local storage. Clearing site data will remove your local ID — your server-side entries remain linked to that UUID but will no longer be accessible from your browser.

## Crisis Support

This app is not a licensed therapist. If you are in distress, please contact Tele-MANAS (14416) or KIRAN (1800-599-0019).
